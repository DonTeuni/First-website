Canvas is a learning management system. It is a user-friendly, complete learning environment that supports teachers and students in teaching and learning. This learning platform should ensure that blended learning is optimally supported digitally within the HU. You can think of finding the course offer, collaborating with the teachers and fellow students remotely, video sharing, asking for feedback from your teacher or using a quiz module to activate knowledge, test and give feedback.

Eduarte brings together learning and testing, supervision and accountability, management and facilitation. We are therefore closer than ever to achieving an ideal application landscape. The Eduarte concept provides freedom in combining service modules with each other or with systems of others. Eduarte is part of Topicus. Students know where they stand. We provide an overview in planning, homework, internships and results. We work together with users towards the same goal: Study success for all students. With Eduarte you have your administration in order and management is well informed with unambiguous and reliable management information. Because we offer everything a teacher needs in one online environment, we let the teachers teach and guide. What they are good at.
